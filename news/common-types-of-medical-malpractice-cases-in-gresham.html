Common Types of Medical Malpractice Cases in Gresham

Common Types of Medical Malpractice Cases in Gresham

Posted by on 2024-10-29

Medical malpractice cases can be devastating for patients and their families. In Gresham, common types of medical malpractice cases include misdiagnosis, surgical errors, medication errors, and birth injuries.

 

Misdiagnosis occurs when a healthcare provider fails to correctly diagnose a patient's condition. This can lead to delayed treatment or unnecessary procedures that can cause harm to the patient. Surgical errors are another common type of medical malpractice in Gresham. These errors can include performing the wrong procedure, operating on the wrong body part, or leaving surgical instruments inside a patient's body.

 

 

Medication errors are also prevalent in Gresham medical malpractice cases. These errors can occur when a healthcare provider prescribes the wrong medication or dosage, leading to adverse reactions or complications for the patient. Birth injuries are another significant concern in medical malpractice cases in Gresham. These injuries can result from negligence during labor and delivery, such as failing to monitor fetal distress or using excessive force during delivery.

 

 

Overall, medical malpractice cases in Gresham encompass a range of issues that can have serious consequences for patients. It is crucial for patients to seek legal representation if they believe they have been the victim of medical malpractice in order to hold healthcare providers accountable and seek justice for any harm caused.
